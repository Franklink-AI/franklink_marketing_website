# Privacy Policy for Franklink

**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025
**Effective Date:** November 7, 2025

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Franklink** ("we," "our," or "us"). Franklink is an AI-powered professional networking and career assistance service that helps users discover opportunities and connect with the right people at the right time.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information transparently and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Franklink through iMessage, our website, or related services (the **"Service"**).

By using Franklink, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use Franklink, you may provide:

- **Phone Number** – used for iMessage communication and account identification
- **Name** – for personalization
- **School Email Address (.edu)** – required for authentication and verification
- **Career Interests & Goals** – information you share during onboarding or conversations
- **Academic Information** – school, major, graduation year, coursework (if shared)
- **Messages** – conversations with Franklink's AI via iMessage

---

### 2.2 Information Collected Through Google OAuth

When you connect your Google account, and **only with your explicit consent**, we may collect:

- **Email Address** – used to verify eligibility and identity
- **Gmail Content (Read-Only)** – processed transiently to identify career- or collaboration-related context (e.g., internships, job postings, events, deadlines)
- **Google Calendar Access** – used only to create reminders or events you explicitly request
- **Profile Information** – name and profile photo for personalization

> **Important:** We do not store raw Gmail content. Email data is processed ephemerally and discarded immediately after extracting relevant signals.

---

### 2.3 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data** – timestamps, interaction flow, feature usage
- **Technical Data** – device type, operating system, iMessage version
- **Log Data** – server logs, diagnostics, and error reports

---

### 2.4 Information from Public Sources

- **Public Web Content** – career opportunities or events from publicly accessible websites or forums
- **URLs You Share** – job postings, event pages, or resources you explicitly send to Franklink

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Provide and Improve the Service

We use your information to:

- Deliver personalized career guidance and professional recommendations
- Identify relevant opportunities, deadlines, and events
- Understand professional context (e.g., interviews, hackathons, exams, applications)
- Facilitate introductions or group conversations **only with your consent**
- Improve service quality and usability using anonymized analytics

> **We do not use Gmail or Calendar data to train AI models.**

---

### 3.2 Matching and Introductions

Franklink may use **derived, non-verbatim signals** (e.g., "looking for a teammate," "upcoming interview," "shared event attendance") to suggest connections or group chats.

- We **never share your email content** with other users
- We **never expose inbox-derived data** directly to others
- Introductions are based on inferred needs and interests, not shared private messages

---

### 3.3 Communication

- Career alerts and reminders via iMessage
- Verification and security emails
- Support responses and service notifications

---

### 3.4 Google Integration Features

- **Gmail Reading:** Scan for career- or collaboration-related signals
- **Email Sending:** Send verification codes or requested notifications
- **Calendar Events:** Create reminders only when you ask

---

### 3.5 Legal and Security Purposes

- Enforce Terms of Service
- Prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We **do not sell** your personal information.

### 4.1 Service Providers

We may share limited information with trusted providers:

- **Photon** – iMessage delivery and messaging infrastructure
- **Supabase** – encrypted database hosting
- **AI Service Providers (e.g., Azure OpenAI)** – conversation processing (excluding Gmail and Calendar data)
- **Google** – OAuth, Gmail, and Calendar APIs
- **Sentry** – anonymized error monitoring

All providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only to provide services on our behalf.

---

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect safety, rights, or security.

---

### 4.3 Business Transfers

If Franklink undergoes a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user data may be transferred with notice.

---

### 4.4 With Your Consent

We may share information only when you explicitly consent.

---

## 5. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Franklink's use of Google user data complies with the **Google API Services User Data Policy**, including **Limited Use requirements**.

### 5.1 What We Do With Google Data

✔ Scan Gmail to identify career- or collaboration-related opportunities
✔ Create calendar events you request
✔ Send verification or security emails

### 5.2 What We Do NOT Do

✗ Do not store raw Gmail content
✗ Do not sell or rent Google data
✗ Do not use Google data for advertising
✗ Do not train AI models on Gmail or Calendar data
✗ Do not allow human review without explicit consent
✗ Do not use Google data outside Franklink's user-facing features

---

### 5.3 Data Minimization & Retention

- **Gmail Content:** Not stored (processed transiently only)
- **Extracted Signals:** Stored up to 1 year or until deleted
- **OAuth Tokens:** Stored securely until revoked
- **Calendar Events:** Stored only in your Google Calendar

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

### 6.1 Storage Locations

- Supabase (U.S.-hosted, encrypted)
- Redis (temporary session data)
- Google (OAuth tokens, calendar events)

---

### 6.2 Security Measures

- TLS/HTTPS encryption in transit
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- OAuth 2.0 with state validation
- Least-privilege internal access
- Regular security reviews

---

### 6.3 Retention

- Active accounts: retained while active
- Inactive accounts: deleted after 2 years
- Conversation history: up to 1 year
- Logs: 90 days
