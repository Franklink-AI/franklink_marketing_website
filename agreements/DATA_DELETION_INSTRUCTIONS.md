# Data Deletion Instructions for Franklink Users

**Last Updated**: November 7, 2025

---

## Overview

This document explains how to delete your data from Franklink and what data will be removed. We are committed to giving you full control over your personal information.

---

## Quick Deletion Options

### Option 1: Via iMessage (Fastest)
1. Open iMessage
2. Message "delete my account" to Franklink
3. Confirm when prompted
4. You'll receive confirmation within 24 hours

### Option 2: Via Email
1. Email: privacy@franklink.ai
2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
3. Include: Your phone number used with Franklink
4. We'll respond within 48 hours

### Option 3: Revoke OAuth Only
If you only want to disconnect your Google account but keep using Franklink:

1. Visit: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
2. Find "Franklink Career Agent"
3. Click "Remove Access"
4. Your OAuth tokens will be immediately revoked

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will delete:

### Immediately Deleted (Within 24 Hours):
- ✓ Personal profile information (name, school, major, interests)
- ✓ Phone number
- ✓ School email address
- ✓ LinkedIn profile URL
- ✓ Conversation history and messages
- ✓ Career preferences and saved opportunities
- ✓ OAuth tokens (Google access revoked)
- ✓ Calendar sync settings

### Deleted Within 30 Days:
- ✓ Anonymized usage analytics
- ✓ Cached recommendation data
- ✓ Session data and temporary files
- ✓ Archived conversation logs

### Permanently Deleted (Cannot Be Recovered):
- All of the above data is permanently deleted and cannot be restored
- If you want to use Franklink again, you'll need to start fresh

---

## What We Retain (Legal & Security)

We may retain the following for legal compliance and security purposes:

### Retained for 90 Days:
- Error logs (anonymized, no personal data)
- Security incident reports
- Fraud prevention records

### Retained Indefinitely (If Required by Law):
- Transaction records (if you made payments)
- Legal compliance records (subpoenas, court orders)
- Abuse/fraud reports

**Note**: Retained data is anonymized when possible and not used for marketing or service improvements.

---

## Third-Party Data Deletion

### Google (Gmail & Calendar)
When you revoke OAuth access or delete your account:

- **Franklink's Access**: Immediately revoked
- **Your Google Data**: Remains in your Google account (we don't store Gmail content)
- **Calendar Events**: Events created by Franklink will remain in your calendar (you can manually delete them)

To delete data stored by Google:
1. Visit: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy
2. Select "Delete a Google service or your account"

### SendBlue (iMessage Delivery)
- SendBlue may retain message metadata for their own compliance
- See SendBlue Privacy Policy: [SendBlue Policy Link]

### Microsoft Azure OpenAI
- We send conversation context to Azure OpenAI for AI responses
- Azure's data retention: 30 days maximum (per their policy)
- No long-term storage by Azure

## After Deletion

### What Happens:
- You will no longer receive messages from Franklink
- Your data will not be used for recommendations or analytics
- You cannot recover your account or data
- If you message Franklink again, a new account will be created

### What Doesn't Happen:
- We don't delete your Google account (only our access to it)
- We don't delete your LinkedIn profile (only our reference to it)
- We don't delete calendar events already created (you manage those)

## Partial Deletion Options

### Option A: Delete OAuth Only
If you want to keep using Franklink but disconnect Google:

1. Visit: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
2. Revoke "Franklink Career Agent" access
3. Continue using basic features without Gmail/Calendar

### Option B: Pause Account (Future Feature)
We're working on an "opt-out" feature that:

- Stops all proactive messages
- Keeps your account for reactivation
- Deletes non-essential data

Message "pause account" to request early access.

## Special Cases

### If You're Under Investigation
If your account is involved in a legal investigation or abuse report:

- Deletion may be delayed pending resolution
- We'll notify you if this applies
- Required data will be retained only as long as legally necessary

### If You Have a School Partnership
If your school has a partnership with Franklink:

- Your school may have separate data retention policies
- We'll coordinate deletion with your school's requirements
- Some anonymized usage data may be retained for institutional reporting

### If You Made Payments (Future)
If paid features are introduced:

- Financial records may be retained for 7 years (tax/legal requirements)
- Personal identifiers will be anonymized after 90 days
- You can request a data report before deletion

## Data Portability (Export Before Deletion)

Before deleting your account, you can request a copy of your data:

### Request Your Data:
1. Message "export my data" via iMessage
2. Or email: privacy@franklink.ai with "Data Export Request"

### What You'll Receive:
- JSON file with your profile information
- List of career opportunities you engaged with
- Conversation history (sanitized)
- OAuth scope authorizations

### Timeline:
- Data export provided within 7 days
- Download link expires after 30 days

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does deletion take?
A: Most data is deleted within 24 hours. Complete deletion takes up to 30 days.

### Q: Can I recover my account after deletion?
A: No. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

### Q: Will my Google account be affected?
A: No. We only revoke Franklink's access. Your Google account remains intact.

### Q: Do I need to delete calendar events manually?
A: Yes. Events created by Franklink will remain in your calendar. You can delete them in Google Calendar.

### Q: What if I just want to stop receiving messages?
A: Message "stop" or "unsubscribe". You'll stop getting proactive messages but can still message us.

### Q: Can my school still see my data after deletion?
A: No. We delete all personal data, including any shared with your school (if applicable).

### Q: What happens to my career opportunity history?
A: Deleted permanently. Export your data first if you want to keep it.

### Q: Will deletion affect my Gmail or Calendar?
A: No. We only remove our access. Your emails and calendar remain unchanged.
