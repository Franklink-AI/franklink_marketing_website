Why we built Franklink

College students today don't have a shortage of tools. They have Handshake, LinkedIn, job boards, event calendars, email lists, and endless advice threads. What they're missing is something more human: a steady companion that sits beside them through the late-night scrolling, the awkward networking events, and the silent wait for interview results.

Franklink started with a simple observation: most students don't get stuck because they lack information. They get stuck because they're overwhelmed, unsure what to do next, or too anxious to even start. The hardest part isn't clicking "submit"—it's deciding what to submit, when, and why.

We built Franklink to feel less like another dashboard and more like a trusted friend who happens to know the career world inside out. It lives where you already talk to the people you trust most—inside iMessage—and it shows up exactly when you need it, not just when you remember to log in.

Our philosophy

Behind every feature in Franklink are a few simple beliefs about what students really need.

Proactive, not reactive

Most tools wait for you to type a question into a search bar. Franklink watches the shape of your week—your events, deadlines, and inbox—and steps in before you hit the panic point.

Companion, not just a tool

Careers are emotional. Rejections, self-doubt, comparison—they all live right beside your calendar. Franklink is designed to answer both "What do I do?" and "How do I get through this?"

Full journey, not single milestones

From undecided first-year to invited-back alumni speaker, your path isn't a one-time decision. Franklink stays with you across exploration, action, setbacks, and wins—learning with you the entire way.

What makes Franklink different

More than search. More than reminders. An anticipatory career companion that stays one step ahead.

Anticipatory guidance

Franklink pays attention to your context—your major, interests, events you've attended, and even how you felt about them. Instead of asking, "What are you looking for?", it suggests relevant opportunities, resources, and next steps before you'd think to search.

Exploration that actually narrows the noise

Early on, Franklink nudges you toward a variety of events, clubs, and workshops. After each one, it asks for quick, low-friction feedback in chat and uses that to get more specific. Over time, your feed shifts from "everything happening on campus" to "this feels like it was designed for me."

Auto-apply and prep, right in chat

When Franklink spots a fit—a fellowship, internship, or program—it can pre-fill your information, remind you of the deadline, and ask if you'd like it to submit on your behalf. It then helps you prepare: mock interviews, tailored talking points, and follow-up messages, all without leaving iMessage.

Inbox- and calendar-aware

Franklink connects with your email and calendar (with your permission) to track interview results, coffee chats, and deadlines. It knows when decisions are likely to land, when follow-ups are due, and when your future self will thank you for a gentle reminder.

Emotional support built in

A string of rejections, a ghosted coffee chat, a relationship that didn't work out—these moments can derail momentum. Franklink notices the hard days and responds with empathy, perspective, and small next steps, not generic advice.

Native to how you already live

There's no new portal to remember, no app to dig for. Franklink is an iMessage contact. You text it like you'd text a close friend, and it quietly handles the heavy lifting in the background.

From first-day nerves to alumni stage lights

Franklink isn't just there for one decision—it walks the whole path with you.

Y1

Year 1 — Exploration without the panic

You arrive on campus surrounded by majors, clubs, and acronyms you've never heard of. Franklink checks in with you, suggests a few low-stakes events and clubs to try, and helps you notice which ones feel right rather than just look impressive.

Y2

Year 2 — Testing interests in the real world

As patterns emerge, Franklink guides you toward projects, research, and roles that deepen your strongest sparks of interest. It helps you say "no" to distractions so you can say "yes" to what actually moves you forward.

Y3

Year 3 — Opportunities, coffee chats, and momentum

Applications accelerate. Franklink auto-fills forms, lines up coffee chats with alumni, and organizes your calendars so you can focus on showing up prepared—not on hunting for links.

Y4

Year 4 — Interviews and decisions

You're juggling interviews, assignments, and goodbyes. Franklink runs mock interviews with you, tracks every "We regret to inform you" and "Congratulations!", and helps you process what each answer really means for your path.

+

Beyond graduation — Returning as the alum

When the right role lands and you step into your first big opportunity, Franklink doesn't disappear. It's still there when you get invited back to speak, mentor a younger student, or navigate your next pivot.

Inside the Franklink system

Under the hood, Franklink is a layered system designed to understand, act, and support—often before you even realize you need it.

Understanding Layer
Builds a living picture of your journey: interests, skills, commitments, feedback on past events, and how you reacted to wins and losses.
Action Layer
Turns understanding into concrete steps: suggesting events, drafting cold emails, scheduling coffee chats, pre-filling applications, and setting up reminders in your calendar.
Memory Layer
Tracks your applications, interviews, outcomes, and reflections over time so Franklink can recognize progress you might overlook and patterns you might miss.
Support Layer
Detects moments of stress, burnout, or disappointment from your interactions and offers encouragement, reframing, or a gentler next step instead of more pressure.
Integration Layer
Connects securely with the tools you already use—iMessage, email, calendars, and campus event sources—so Franklink can stay in sync with your real life, not just your intentions.

When you don't know where to start

It's the first week of school. Everyone seems to have a five-year plan. When someone asks what you're doing in the future, all you can say is, "I'm… undecided." That night, as you scroll through a dozen open tabs, Franklink checks in.

"Still up? You've been working hard. Time to rest. We can explore a few options tomorrow—together."

Over the next few weeks, it nudges you toward a handful of events and clubs. One of them feels unexpectedly like home.

[Image: Student closing laptop after video call, phone showing iMessage notification, coffee cup nearby, campus quad visible through window]

When opportunity is one "yes" away

You've just finished a mock interview with Franklink on your laptop. It sends you a breakdown of what went well and what to refine. As you close the call, a new message appears.

"By the way, that program you liked? The application deadline is tomorrow. I've pre-filled your info—type YES if you want me to submit."

You type "YES" on your phone, and Franklink takes care of the logistics while you head out to a coffee chat with an alum it helped you schedule.

[Image: Evening scene, student looking at phone with mixed emotions, email inbox visible with multiple messages, warm lamp light]

When nine rejections land at once

One evening, you refresh your inbox and see a wall of "Thank you for your application" emails. On your tracking sheet, the rejection column fills in faster than you'd like to admit. Before you can put words to how it feels, Franklink asks:

"Hey, how did everything go today?"

When you answer honestly, it doesn't pretend everything is fine. It acknowledges the loss, reminds you how far you've already come, and suggests one small next step. Moments later, a new subject line appears: "Congratulations…" Franklink is right there for that moment too.
